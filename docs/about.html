Welcome to Little Flower Higher Secondary School

Established in 1989, Little Flower Higher Secondary School has stood as a beacon of academic excellence and moral education in the Kiphire district of Nagaland. Nestled in a serene rural setting, we offer a warm, nurturing environment where students from Nursery to Grade 10 are prepared not only academically, but also spiritually and socially.

We follow a co-educational, English-medium curriculum designed to build both intellectual and emotional intelligence in every child. Our infrastructure supports modern learning techniques, while our dedicated faculty ensures each student receives personal attention. Our Mission

To foster a transformative and holistic education that empowers every child to become a responsible, knowledgeable, and empathetic global citizen. We strive to ignite a lifelong passion for learning, rooted in discipline and moral values.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence in the region, producing future leaders who are academically sound, emotionally resilient, spiritually grounded, and socially responsible.
